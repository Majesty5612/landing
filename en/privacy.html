Privacy Policy

InstVPN Service takes the protection of its users' privacy seriously. This Policy describes what data we collect, how we use and protect it.

1. Data Handling Principles

In providing our services, we adhere to the following principles:

  • We do not log user actions, traffic, or message content.
  • We do not transfer data to third parties.
  • We do not restrict internet connection or access to internet resources.
  • We do not store traffic consumption data broken down by days and devices.

2. Data Encryption

All traffic on our servers is encrypted using modern encryption protocols. We use reliable algorithms to ensure maximum protection of your data.

3. Technical Data Collection

We perform anonymous collection of error data solely to improve service quality. Collected data includes:

  • Error information
  • Date of occurrence
  • Device operating system

We do not store user IP addresses.

4. Registration and Identification

To use InstVPN service, only a Telegram account is required. We do not request or store personal data such as full name, residential address, or passport details.

Telegram ID is used solely for subscription identification and does not allow establishing user identity.

5. Payment Data

Payment processing is carried out through secure payment systems. InstVPN does not have access to users' bank card data.

Payment systems are authorized to process payment data with a prohibition on using personal information for any purposes other than providing payment services.

6. Server Locations

Our servers are located in jurisdictions with reliable data protection: Netherlands, Germany, Sweden, and other European countries. We choose locations that ensure maximum user privacy.

7. Log Storage

InstVPN adheres to a strict no-logs policy:

  • We do not keep connection logs
  • We do not track visited websites
  • We do not record connection and disconnection times
  • We do not save data transfer volumes by users

8. Interaction with Third Parties

We do not cooperate with government agencies and do not transfer any data upon request, as we do not have information that could be provided.

9. Cookies

Our website may use a minimal amount of technical cookies for proper operation. We do not use tracking cookies or analytics tools that collect personal data.

10. Policy Changes

We reserve the right to review and change this Privacy Policy at any time. The current version is always available on this page.
